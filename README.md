# House of Dreams · Are Vananga o Oneunga

> An off-grid, self-sufficient **village and learning campus** on family land at
> **Oneunga, Mauke**, Cook Islands — a place where, freed from the everyday costs of food,
> energy, and housing, people can come, stay, and discover and live out their purpose.

## What it is

A modular campus built from timber **"petal"** modules (POU Architecture). Each petal is
≈ **45 m²** — a ~4° fan-shaped slice, ~9 m radius, elevated on posts, with a curved roof and
integrated amphitheatre steps. Petals cluster into part-circles to form larger spaces.

**Central hub:** commercial kitchen & dining · marae entrance + amphitheatre stage ·
classrooms · workshop (canoe building, carving, innovation) · toilets & showers ·
ocean-facing decks.

**Wider masterplan:** café, hall/marae, villa accommodation, cultural centre, food forest,
basketball court, workshop, view points, and a beach boardwalk — with existing burial
grounds respected. The **café and villa accommodation** are designed as built-in income
streams that help sustain the campus.

## Status

Concept design and master planning are **complete**. The near-term goal is to build the
first **1–2 petals** as the first visible step on the land.
